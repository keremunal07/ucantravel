- Turkish Night

Enjoy an authentic Turkish cultural experience with this exciting dinner and belly dance show! After you are picked up from your hotel, enjoy delicious traditional Turkish food from an open buffet and enjoy your choice of drinks. The Turkish Night Show highlights the dance traditions of various regions of Turkey, complete with traditional costumes and of course, exhilarating belly dancers. This lively performance is a great way to enjoy traditional Turkish music and dancing, and even join in the festivities during audience participation. A fabulous Turkish dinner and a spectacular floorshow with bejeweled belly dancers, the ultimate Turkish experience!
